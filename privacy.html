Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026 · Last updated: May 15, 2026

VELA ("we", "us", or "our") operates the expedition intelligence application available at app.vela-go.com and the website at vela-go.com . This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

By using VELA you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the VELA service:

  • Google account information — your name, email address, and profile photo, collected when you sign in with Google OAuth.
  • Location data — your GPS coordinates, used only in-session to provide weather, fire alerts, and air quality information for your location. We do not store your location history.
  • Trip data — trip names, dates, regions, and waypoints you create within the app.
  • Gear registry data — equipment items, categories, conditions, and notes you enter.
  • Device usage data — EcoFlow device readings and Starlink status data. This data is fetched from your personal devices and is not stored on our servers beyond your session.
  • Subscription data — billing information is handled entirely by Stripe. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the VELA service
  • To authenticate your identity via Google OAuth
  • To display relevant safety, weather, and environmental data for your location
  • To sync your trips and gear across your devices
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To provide AI-powered features using the Anthropic Claude API (PRO plan only)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. AI features and your data

VELA PRO includes AI features powered by the Anthropic Claude API. When you use the Survival Agent, Knowledge Base, or Meal Planning features:

  • Your messages and queries are sent to Anthropic's API for processing
  • Your current GPS location, weather, and air quality data may be included as context to provide relevant answers
  • Your gear registry summary may be included to provide equipment-specific advice
  • Your Anthropic API key is stored encrypted in our database and is never transmitted in plaintext
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. See anthropic.com/privacy

4. Data storage and security

  • Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled — you can only access your own data
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • Sensitive values (API keys) are encrypted before storage
  • We do not store payment card information — all payments are processed by Stripe
  • GPS location data is processed in your browser and is not permanently stored

5. Third-party services

VELA uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google OAuth — authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI features (PRO only). Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • NIFC / NOAA / AirNow — public government data APIs. No personal data is shared with these services.
  • EcoFlow API — your device telemetry. EcoFlow Privacy Policy

6. Data retention

  • Your account data is retained while your account is active
  • If you delete your account all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Trip and gear data can be deleted by you at any time within the app
  • Subscription records are retained as required by law for financial compliance

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights contact us at dan@vela-go.com

8. Children's privacy

VELA is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of VELA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy contact us at:

  • Email: dan@vela-go.com
  • Website: vela-go.com
