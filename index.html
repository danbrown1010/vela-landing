What VELA does

Everything you need out there.

Built on real data from NIFC, NOAA, AirNow, and your own EcoFlow devices. Works offline when you need it most.

Fire & safety alerts

Live NIFC fire perimeters, evacuation zones, AQI, and fire weather warnings — all on one map. Know before you go.

EcoFlow monitoring

Real-time battery SOC, input/output wattage, port control, and time remaining across all your EcoFlow devices simultaneously.

Offline trip maps

MapLibre with OpenFreeMap tiles that work without cell signal. Plan routes, mark waypoints, and navigate in the backcountry.

Gear registry

Track every piece of kit — condition, quantity, vendor, purchase link. Feeds into your pre-trip packing checklist automatically.

Weather & conditions

NOAA weather, AirNow AQI, fire weather watches, and red flag warnings — all pulled for your exact GPS location.

Starlink monitoring

Dish status, download/upload speeds, latency, and obstruction percentage — live from your Starlink via local proxy.

PRO · AI features

Intelligence for the field.

Powered by Claude — the same AI that understands context, nuance, and the stakes of decisions made miles from help.

Survival Agent

A wilderness expert that knows your GPS location, current weather, AQI, and active trip. Ask about recovery, first aid, fire behavior, water treatment, or mechanical diagnosis — and get answers calibrated to your actual situation.

Knowledge Base

Upload your Jeep manual, Ursa Minor guide, EcoFlow docs, and recovery handbooks. Ask anything — get answers with exact page references from your own documents.

Meal Planning

Tell it your trip length, party size, cooking setup, and dietary needs. Get a complete day-by-day meal plan with shopping list, prep instructions, and calorie counts — optimised for off-grid cooking.
